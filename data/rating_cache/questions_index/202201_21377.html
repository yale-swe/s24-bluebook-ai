\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1589645\n 1589645\n \n 1589645\n I loved this class. Top 2 of the courses I have taken at Yale. \r\nStrength - Commenting and discussions with Persuall is fabulous. City documentary is amazing as well

\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1594382\n 1594382\n \n 1594382\n I think the course is structured very well - there are a good amount of drafts required (forcing you to think about what you are writing earlier), and if not a draft, a roundtable. Prof. Grogan was very accessible and replied to emails quickly. I think some of the texts could be a bit more interesting, though I understand why they were picked (I also will note I thought most texts were quite interesting). I also think grading could be improved: we got our essays back quite late, and the feedback offered was sometimes quite valuable for future essays.
