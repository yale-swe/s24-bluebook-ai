\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1713242\n 1713242\n \n 1713242\n The strengths of the course are that Maria is such an amazing instructor. One weakness is that we learn everything through the projects we paint throughout the course, and I would have appreciated some more beginner guidelines on just understanding color and light, etc. before jumping into the projects.
\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1714338\n 1714338\n \n 1714338\n Strengths of this course include the access to materials, open ended assignments, and welcoming/supportive work and critique environment. Another would be that for people with no painting experience at all it was difficult to understand how to use materials in the beginning; this class would benefit from a "crash-course" session for people who need an intro to things like how to use medium, best brush practices, mixing colors, etc.
\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1721825\n 1721825\n \n 1721825\n I think that a strength of the course was that it encouraged exploration and non-traditional approaches to painting. As a course for students without much experience painting, it was not overly critical or restrictive and allowed for everyone to experiment with personal and unique styles. I think a weakness could potentially be that there was sometimes limited time left for crits, so it was difficult to receive more in-depth feedback during class crits.
\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1722762\n 1722762\n \n 1722762\n The strengths in this course would be towards the professor and her engagement in the classroom. I feel as though she got to really understand not necessarily every student as a person, but as an artist. She payed attention to the little details in our art styles and processes we didn't even notice and in doing so she was able to guide us through the different prompts in a way that genuinely benefited our interests and needed and didn't feel pressuring in any way. The assignments themselves were also very experimental and allowed a lot of breathing room to engage in different styles, methods, and painting mediums. A weakness in the course was perhaps the timeline. Although very generously, our assignments were due based on adjusted due dates that benefited our completion time, in the end this proved to hinder our ability to throughly finish our final projects. I feel as though now with a better understanding of the average time it takes to finish the pieces, the timeline could be better organized to work up towards the final project and critique in a way that can satisfy majority of the class.
