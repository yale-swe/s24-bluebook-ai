\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1543064\n 1543064\n \n 1543064\n I was able to greatly improve my speaking skills in Spanish and the level of conversation I was able to have in the language deepened.
\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1544773\n 1544773\n \n 1544773\n This course taught me many small details about the grammar of the Spanish language that I have never before learned- these grammatical changes have bettered my speaking and writing abilities, especially. This course gave me the ability to deconstruct my speaking of Spanish and analyze it, while also reinforcing the memorization of concepts that are used constantly so that I can rely on having innately correct grammar.
