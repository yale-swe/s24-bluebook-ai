\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1667873\n 1667873\n \n 1667873\n What I liked most in this course was how much it encouraged the students to interact with one another to practice what was covered in class. Instead of limiting the topics in grammar and the discussions of cultural specialties of a country (Germany, in this case) to presentations prepared for the instructor by someone else in the faculty, the relevant instructor could have more freedom to customize the curriculum of the course.

I felt that a lot of the work did not help my skills at all.
