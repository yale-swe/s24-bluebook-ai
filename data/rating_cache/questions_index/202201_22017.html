I particularly loved the opportunity for us to bring in outside connections to replace our discussion posts; I learned so much from my classmates over the course of the term. The weakness of this course, however, was the scope of its material. Because we covered such a large geographical area and time period, sometimes it was more difficult to understand the nuances of what we were reading because we did not have any background information. While Professor Eller's further reading suggestions were incredibly helpful, I think that it would be nice to incorporate some crash course, mini-lectures in the future in order to provide quick context to students--particularly those who aren't history students or who are unfamiliar with Latin America and its history.
