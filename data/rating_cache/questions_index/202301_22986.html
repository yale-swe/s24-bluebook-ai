\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1649353\n 1649353\n \n 1649353\n I learned about the major artists and artworks of the Netherlands through the Dutch Republic, as well as the gender dynamics, political climate, religious conflicts, and social considerations which formed the backdrop for the production of incredible artworks throughout the 17th century Netherlands. In this art history class moreso than others, we explored the colonial relationships that governed consumption and production of "exotic" goods and flora/fauna which constituted a large part of Dutch art inspired by the colonies. I also learned about the development of studying life through microscopes, the group portrait, trompe l'oeil, dollhouses, and many other art forms. I gained new entrypoints into analyzing paintings and other artworks throughout the semester through section, and was encouraged to go outside the box for my final paper.
