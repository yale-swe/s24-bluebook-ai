\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1620749\n 1620749\n \n 1620749\n Yes, I would highly recommend this first-year seminar. It is a very unique course that consists entirely of a weekly 3-hour trip to the Furniture Study Center on West Campus where students are immersed in historical furniture. It provides a completely new perspective to students and attracts a great, diverse group. Other than the readings, which simply help to inform the student presentations of each week, there is very little work assigned (three short papers, three oral presentations, and a final project).
