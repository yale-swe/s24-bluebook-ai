\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1723567\n 1723567\n \n 1723567\n If you're doing DS, I absolutely recommend McGowan's section. He is an incredibly engaging, helpful, and caring professor.\r\nIf the question is doing DS in general: I'm glad I did it (I'm continuing in the spring), but only take it if it fits well with your academic goals. I came into DS thinking it would just be a good way of making myself read a bunch of books that I wouldn't get to otherwise and that it would give me a nice footing to read and study the books I'm actually interested in. Throughout the semester I've realized that 1) if you're seriously studying literature this definitely won't be the last time you engage with or read most of these texts and 2) there are plenty of reasons you'll want to come back to them.
