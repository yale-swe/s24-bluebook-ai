Lectures and problem sets were occasionally cancelled on short notice with no real backup plan, so it sometimes felt we did not cover or practice important concepts before exams. For the final, it was incredibly unclear what we would be responsible for knowing; each of the teaching staff had different answers and many of the topics were removed over time, making the practice problems we actually were provided irrelevant. With all of that said, grading (at least on the midterm) was very fair given the class distribution. The TAs were great and definitely trying their best to help us. I'm not sure I would take this class again over 440 if I knew how disorganized it would end up being.
