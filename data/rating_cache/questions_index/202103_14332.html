\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1532249\n 1532249\n \n 1532249\n This was a great course with many strengths. It was a great introduction to philosophy, giving you a pretty deep look into the thoughts of all of these brilliant old philosophers. Classes were also fun, being focused on debating the ideas, but this, I believe, was the one weakness of the class. I wish that the professor had used more of the time to explain his own thoughts on the topic, which he had developed over decades, rather than just allowing us to use the whole class debating the points ourselves.
