\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1514308\n 1514308\n \n 1514308\n The biggest strengths of this course are the instructors, particularly our TF, Kate. Kate was incredibly kind and understanding, and she was very passionate about the subject. She lead class discussions really well, and they were always organized effectively. I really enjoyed getting to learn from her as well as Professor Dunham, especially about their own research projects and interests. The structure of the class was also really good, and I liked how open to feedback Kate and Professor Dunham were, so we modified things as the course progressed. The only weakness of the class in my opinion is that some of the secondary popular press articles felt somewhat out of place.
