Psets were not too difficult, you could usually figure them out using the textbook and your notes, and they usually only took a few hours a week which was pretty nice. The class wasn't a huge timesuck on a weekly basis, but you definitely have to focus a lot on studying for the midterms/final. I studied an insane amount for the midterm and did decently, but it took me forever to relearn all of the content since I didn't understand it too well when I initially learned it. The class didn't do too well; we averaged a 57/100, and she decided not to curve it, instead curving the class and offering extra credit (5% of our course grade for going on a field trip to a power plant). I'd say the class is solidly mid, but if you're taking this it's probably required for your major. Not the most interesting/well taught class, but definitely not the most cursed one out there.
