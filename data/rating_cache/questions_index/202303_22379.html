\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1715379\n 1715379\n \n 1715379\n The most impactful thing I learned from this course is the nuances of the Japanese language and the effects that isolation can have on the complexities of the words and script. I also found the unique combination of both buddhist and shinto beliefs to be a fascinating mixture of religions where neither dominated the other.
