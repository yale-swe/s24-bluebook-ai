In addition to the language, we were exposed to the standard poetic form of OE, the linguistic history of OE (e.g. Verner's Law, Grimm's Law, Anglo-Frisian Brightening), elements of comparative linguistics, the manuscript traditions of certain OE texts, and editing texts from manuscripts (one of the 3 options for the final project).
