It also taught me more about the US legal system through the comparison to other countries, particularly on areas where the United States is an outlier (e.g., free speech). I also enjoyed writing the book review and the final paper. Weaknesses: I think the course might have benefited from a weekly writing assignment to gather our thinking before class. I also enjoyed reading and review Professor Ackerman's book Revolutionary Constitutions. My only criticisms are that I wish we received more specific feedback on our midterm papers as well as more specific instruction on what to write for the midterm and final--the prompts were quite vague, and the lack of a grading rubric or model papers meant I had to ask Professor Calabresi for clarification on expectations quite a few times during office hours.
