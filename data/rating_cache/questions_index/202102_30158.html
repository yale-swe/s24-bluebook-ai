\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1512483\n 1512483\n \n 1512483\n This is more a symptom of the brevity of the course, but as someone who was doing full-time work on top of the copious amounts of material being covered in class, I found myself struggling to keep up with assignments in a timely manner. \n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1512527\n 1512527\n \n 1512527\n Strengths- Overall course structure. For as much time as I put into this course, I never really felt overwhelmed or frustrated. By using our discussion posts to facilitate the live discussion section, Robinson really tailored the course to our classs interests while still covering the material he wanted to in the recorded lectures.\r\n\r\nWeaknesses- Sheer quantity of material. The class isnt over-the-top difficult, but if youre working or doing research full time, it can often be difficult to finish all the readings. Its not really something that can be changed but especially when a particular day has very long plays assigned its just something you have to plan for. I also wish there was an easier way to access some of the texts, as some are on the Canvas site, but others have to be purchased and some were straight-up unavailable on e-readers, so I was unable to get them in time to actively engage with the discussion in class. I worked full-time while taking this course and the nearest bookstore or library is quite far from where I live so I didnt have time to go hunting for the texts if they werent available somewhere online.
