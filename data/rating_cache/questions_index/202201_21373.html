\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1592746\n 1592746\n \n 1592746\n The strength of this course is clearly Peter Conroy. I can't think of a more engaging and kind teacher for this course. He clearly knew the material and had always had some interesting angle to discuss. Other than that, the class was extremely great!
