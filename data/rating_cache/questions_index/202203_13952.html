Her insight, discussion moderation, care for students, and dedication to teaching shone through most brightly in the class. She was simply a joy to work with and an inspiration every day. The assignments all built in a powerful way and gave me the opportunity to explore a really well-researched final essay I was proud of. Feedback was also a strength of the class.
