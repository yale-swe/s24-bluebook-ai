They expected us to recall the families of a bunch of the plants that we'd worked with throughout the semester, identify and label structures which we'd barely learned about (some of which, I don't think we ever learned the names for, or maybe it was just casually tossed out in class), and a lot of content from the lecture course was tested on in the lab final, despite us not having talked about it specifically in lab.
