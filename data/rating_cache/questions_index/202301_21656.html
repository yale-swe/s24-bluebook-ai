\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1646519\n 1646519\n \n 1646519\n Strengths: relatively relaxed, allows you to focus on your work. Some of the guest speakers are really interesting. \r\n\r\nSome of the classes are on how to get a internship but they are at the end of the spring semester when it is too late to get one. Having these in the fall would be more helpful.
\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1657963\n 1657963\n \n 1657963\n This course did a good job of being hands-off. The research was primarily driven by the student and their advisor and the professor was mostly just there to provide a loose structure. This is helpful as the class is supposed to be focused on the student's individual research and the time spent in the class does not detract from that.
\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1659536\n 1659536\n \n 1659536\n WEAKNESSES: I received essentially no feedback to help me improve as a researcher. There were very few assignments in the course: a prospectus, a midterm report, and a final presentation. For the written assignments, we were given written instructions but little advice on how to write them, and the assignments were graded with no substantive comments. For the final presentation, we were given barely any instructions, and the only feedback was one or two questions about the content, and nothing about the delivery. I think this class could be improved by focusing a lot more on how to write and present well, and by giving us more extensive guidelines and then feedback on the assignments we do have. I also think it would be good to have more regularly scheduled check-ins, to make sure everyone is on track. \r\nSTRENGTHS: I liked some of the guest speakers and panels, especially those on grad school and on OCS.
\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1678143\n 1678143\n \n 1678143\n strengths: we got to choose what we wanted to get out of the course and focus on those things, the course didn't assign much work that took away from my actual research, I really liked seeing what my peers were up to and learning more about different fields on engineering research\r\nweaknesses: the meeting time was a little inconvenient
