The course allows you to experiment with theoretical concepts learned in class and lose, find what works and doesn't, and learn from your mistakes before negotiating in real life. You get used to the competition under pressure with tight deadlines and little time for preparation, which is often the case in real life, with fulfilling outcomes at all times, either in the form of winning or reflecting on your mistakes in the negotiation report that complements each negotiation. \r\nProf. Singer is an entertaining and engaging professor. Dan always stayed in touch and was eager to meet in person to talk about all sorts of things, from expanding on the concepts learned in class to giving life advice. Overall, the seminar created a comfortable environment for growth as an individual.
