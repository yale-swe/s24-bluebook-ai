\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1618591\n 1618591\n \n 1618591\n Strengths - small class and great community. Certainly not a dealbreaker for this incredible course, just kind of annoying.
\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1618699\n 1618699\n \n 1618699\n This course is very strong in providing scaffolding for new learners of ASL. My problems with this class were in organizationit seemed like there were a lot of attempts to standardize between classes and instructors that didnt work/werent appropriate to learning needs/overly complicated our lives. There were so many documents and rubrics and files within files that it was personally inaccessible for me. My instructor did a good job handling this, but it was frustrating.
