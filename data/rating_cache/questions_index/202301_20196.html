This felt like an especially important approach, considering the confusion or gaps in information that often surround conversations on U.S. empire. By the end of the class, I felt like I had already learned a lot, wanted to learn more, and had the tools to seek out, understand, and effectively communicate new information. Since the readings for the class were all primary sources, I would have loved a reading list of secondary sources to check out in the future at the end of class, or maybe suggested secondary source readings throughout. Professor Akiboh mentioned many of these sources in her lectures, but a list or additional sources would have been great too.
