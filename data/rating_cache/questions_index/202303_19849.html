\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1695253\n 1695253\n \n 1695253\n I would definitely recommend this course to another student as I believe that it is crucial to developing a strong writing, speaking, and cultural proficiency in Spanish. Working with my peers in group discussion forums on Canvas was very interesting as we got to share our ideas on art pieces. I also enjoyed the choice of pieces we were given from the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA) in New York to interpret, such as the sculpture of a woman's face or a drawing by a woman painter named Rosalie Bonheur of goats (chevres), or a picture of two women (one pregnant and the other not) clad in blue two pieces next to the pool.
