\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1665940\n 1665940\n \n 1665940\n I appreciated the small size of this course and the opportunities for students to ask questions during lecture. I think it saves time and effort to clarify concepts when they are initially explained, rather than being confused for longer as you try to work on problem sets. I also appreciated the office hours held by the professor himself twice a week. Because of the small class size, these office hours were never overly crowded, and I never left with any of my questions unanswered. Similarly I felt that review sessions before exams provided me with adequate time to ask every single one of my questions. I appreciated the Canvas quizzes at the beginning of the semester because I thought they were a great way to review the material as we learned it and make sure we understood everything. I wish that these had continued throughout the semester.

However, the seminar had a strict curve from the economics department that made the exams extremely important and, as a result, very stressful. Still, the course is worth the work.
