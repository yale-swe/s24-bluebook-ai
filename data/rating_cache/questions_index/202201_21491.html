\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1580929\n 1580929\n \n 1580929\n This course had so many strengths - each unit, discussion topic set, and set of readings was so well thought out. Our discussions were always fascinating, and the readings too. Several of us in class had a bit of difficulty figuring out what the readings were for a given week (high stakes, as we only met once a week) especially towards the beginning. The cause for this was that it was the modules tab in Canvas where this info was being communicated, but we'd been mistakenly looking at the syllabus (a mistake I think many people might have easily made). A bit more emphasis on the importance of the Modules tab early on in the course (and its objectively superior importance than the syllabus) would have helped.\r\n\r\nI also think it would have really improved my learning if we'd had the chance to do re-writes of essays (that is, first and second drafts) for one or both papers I could have delved deeper into a thread with the help of the professor in a way that office hours alone would not have enabled me to.
