It was really cool to learn in a hands-on way how programs can be optimized to make them run efficiently.

In particular, we covered topics such as X86, lexers/parsers, type-checking, optimizations, register allocation, LLVM, etc. The problem sets generally provide an opportunity for you to directly apply these techniques in OCaml, however, we didn't do much problem-solving manually until the final exam.
