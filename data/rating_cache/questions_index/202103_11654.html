\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1536625\n 1536625\n \n 1536625\n This class is a lot of fun - it's a unique chance to play gamelan music and learn from a master of the art. This class could have dedicated more time to understanding the traditional cultural context of gamelan music and how that relates to us playing the music. We only spent one class talking about Java, and we didn't really learn anything about the specific songs we played or where they came from (who wrote them, how they are usually performed, etc).
