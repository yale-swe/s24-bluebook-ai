\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1715969\n 1715969\n \n 1715969\n The content itself is interesting, and so was Prof. Yarmola's teaching style. I especially enjoyed when the class drew comparisons to real world examples of math in action, such as flux in a physics context. All the guided notes were easy to access. Ximera was a great resource. Prof. Yarmola was great at office hours and always take immense time and effort to make sure his students understood a concept.\r\n\r\nHowever, I thought the locations of TA office hours were not posted clearly.
