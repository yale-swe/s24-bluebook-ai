\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1530775\n 1530775\n \n 1530775\n Strengths: LOVED how Dr. Cutter and Dr. Beitel worked together to teach this class!! This is heavy material, and having the two of them made this classroom more of a community. Since we learned from both of them, we got even more information and perspective than we otherwise would have. It was also wonderful when the professors shared their personal experiences as clinical psychologists with us. That info from people who do this as a living actually inspired me to want to switch from a pre-law-esque path to psychology. They also impressed upon us how important it is for more psychology research to look at Native Americans because of all sorts of disparities and the lack of current research on Native populations. The assignments were also very well designed to engage us and the weekly journaling, media analysis paper, and final research paper assignments all served different but important roles in getting us to really learn new things and be excited about what we were learning. \r\nWeaknesses: honestly I don't think there are any weaknesses of the course. I do wish we'd had even more time to discuss things as a class. Dr. Cutter and Dr. Beitel really worked to create a space where we all had to share things in class and felt comfortable doing so (so that's another strength lol). Maybe asking even more discussion-based questions could help facilitate that, but overall I really do not think there are any weaknesses of the course and I seriously hope that this course is taught again and again and again!!!! it's so important
\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1537553\n 1537553\n \n 1537553\n It is amazing to have an opportunity to take a course cross-listed in psych and ER&M. I also was frustrated by the lack of focus on historical contexts of contemporary health experiences. \n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1546553\n 1546553\n \n 1546553\n This is an incredibly important course and I feel so fortunate to have had the opportunity to take it.
