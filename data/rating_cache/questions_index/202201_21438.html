You learn about "studying up," about how the family is an issue of the state, and more.

\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1593780\n 1593780\n \n 1593780\n As a student who hadn't learned a lot about ethnographies, I learned a lottt about ethnographies and the practice of feminist anthropology! The theme for this class this year was family, community, and nation so a major insight was about how notions of kinship can be influenced by nationalist agendas. This course will positively challenge you in all the right ways, I cant recommend it enough. \r\n\r\nProf. Pepi is brilliant and has curated the syllabus so thoughtfully and meticulously.
