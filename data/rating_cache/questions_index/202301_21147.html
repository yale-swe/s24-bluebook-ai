It is relatively dry, and the applications are hard to see in a course that is mainly focused on teaching the abstract concepts. The recitation sessions were very helpful in facilitating understanding of trickier problem set questions. By the end of the course, I feel like I have a strong foundation in basic thermodynamics concepts that are crucial for higher level CENG courses (and Pchem 1).\r\n\r\nWeaknesses: More focus on in-class examples in the earlier sections, especially entropy and heat engines, would be appreciated.
