\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1724427\n 1724427\n \n 1724427\n Lectures were excellent (and particularly entertaining in the case of Prof. De La Cruz's lectures) and the combination of engaging lectures, psets, and discussion sections helped students develop an intuition (aka "molecular empathy") for diffusion and equilibrium binding (Adair's equation) and build a foundation for advanced topics like statistical thermodynamics. Especially in the first half of the course, I felt that we sometimes moved through material rather slowly. I feel like there must be better ways of showing us how to model without all the superfluous functions we had to learn to get to that point. Peer tutor hours could be a bit sparse but at the end of the semester once I could start attending I found them very helpful.
