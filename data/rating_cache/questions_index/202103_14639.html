\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1536823\n 1536823\n \n 1536823\n The course was structured rather well. During the first half of the semester, we focused on different national security issues by geographical locations, while in the second half, we focused on more technical aspects of national security such as nuclear policy and the organizational structure of India's national security apparatus. Professor Singh encouraged us to ask questions and gave good replies to them. The assignments took the form of one midterm paper and a long final paper. We were given a variety of prompts and I mostly enjoyed researching and writing them. There were times when the class felt overly didactic and like a lecture, and student engagement during the seminars was not always active. The readings for the class were interesting; however, I would have liked more substantial academic papers rather than op-eds.
