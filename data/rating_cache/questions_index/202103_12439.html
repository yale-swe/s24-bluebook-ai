\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1545681\n 1545681\n \n 1545681\n Take it! Eva is the best. This was an awesome class. The assignments were 8 psets, 2 podcasts, an in-class midterm, a 7-min presentation on an industry of your choice, a 4-6 page write-up about that industry, and a final. In the first half the class, you learn about how technology has changed/impact the basic economic ideas you've learned in micro and macro. Like for monopolies--think about Google. Like for searching costs--think about online e-commerce. Like for vertical integration and price elasticity--think about Amazon. The second half of the class is about industry analysis. Using Porter's Five Forces framework, each student researches and presents on a topic and there are subsequent class discussions. I liked how presentations were on a wide range of industries: blockchain, streaming, Amazon e-commerce, Tesla electric and autonomous cars, fintech, etc.
