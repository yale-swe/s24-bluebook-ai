Likewise, the advisors should have more structure, as some were far too harsh and expected too much of students (especially for lab work), while others were barely present and allowed students to fall through the cracks with little or no direction. Again, this could come in the form of weekly or biweekly meetings, maybe during the designated class time instead of unengaging lectures. Overall, many changes need to be made to help what is presumably a very skilled and passionate group of students complete suitably engaging and complete projects.
