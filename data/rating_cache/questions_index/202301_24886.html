We explored the complexities of food distribution, identified various players involved, and understood economic and social factors influencing how food gets from the farm to the table.\r\n\r\nThe course also teaches students how to create engaging, concise infographics. Assignments included a memo to the mayor, a map, an analysis of a grocery store, and a farm crop plan.\r\n\r\nBy the end of the course, we had a much deeper understanding of how the food system operates in urban areas and how different components of the system interact with each other. The course will be especially useful for those interested in sustainability, urban planning, or food systems.
