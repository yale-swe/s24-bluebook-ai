\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1551153\n 1551153\n \n 1551153\n This course highlights just how important the humanities are when considering climate change. The humanities relate to how we view nature and the human species as actors in our world. They determine how the public perceives climate change and environmental disaster, and therefore what type of response we can expect. Additionally, climate change humanities relates to how we can develop fair and equitable response to climate change and disaster. This course makes it clear that, although climate science is hugely important, the humanities cannot be discounted when it comes to planning and enacting our climate future.
