\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1581571\n 1581571\n \n 1581571\n The topics covered in this course are incredibly engaging, as they are pulled from a variety of source material and cover a wide variety of topics, including the Russian judicial system, protest, publicity, human rights, and minority rights. The readings are also of manageable length, such that they can be effectively summarized in each week's required discussion post. I also greatly enjoyed how each class session did not only consist of lecturing or discussions, but rather a combination of the two (with occasional presentations by my classmates). While we knew the general topics that were going to be covered, I would have appreciated having a few guiding questions to keep in mind as I read, in order to be able to better connect what I read to what would be covered in class the following week. In this manner, I believe I could have done my readings more effectively, as I could begin them already knowing my professor's expectations, as well as the main takeaways I should get from each reading.
