\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1673284\n 1673284\n \n 1673284\n this course was great and i definitely became a better writer because of it but there are a lot of weaknesses. a lot of the assignments are not specific enough in terms of what they ask you to write... which would be fine, but prof. shirkhani looks for certain things and looks for a specific way of writing a creative essay. we talked a lot about the importance of personal narrative in writing a cultural critique, but towards the second half of the semester it really felt like almost none of the essays were about personal narrative.... so a lot of the model essays studied in class were rendered practically useless in giving us literary techniques to borrow. \r\n\r\nthis isn't so much a weakness... but be prepared to read differently... we didn't really seem to talk about techniques as much when breaking down the readings in the class imo, it felt a lot more like we were breaking down their arguments. \r\n\r\nthe people in the class were cool tho! <3 and prof. shirkhani is really funny
\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1673295\n 1673295\n \n 1673295\n Strengths: Shirkhani is very kind and helpful always. Readings are interesting and enlightening. Workshops are fruitful. \r\nWeaknesses: Having one draft is definitely hard!
\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1673527\n 1673527\n \n 1673527\n Strengths: Professor Shirkhani is an amazing professor and everyone who can take a class with her should absolutely do so. She is so thoughtful in reading paper drafts and meeting with students. Her feedback is truly one of a kind. More than any other professor I have encountered at Yale, I felt that she really thought so deeply about my papers and offered such thought-provoking feedback that pushed my thinking and my writing. She is really really great at picking out ideas in papers and pushing them forward to another line of thought. She also brought up very important ideas in our seminar discussions, encouraging me and other students to examine great writing at the sentence level as inspiration for our own writing. Beyond her wonderful teaching, Professor Shirkhani is a lovely person and her class was always the highlight of my week! The class material was also very well selected and the readings were all on interesting, relevant topics and by great writers. Weaknesses: None!

Since this is a class on Cultural Critique, I felt like the class did not really ever progress into different types or more involved forms of writing.
