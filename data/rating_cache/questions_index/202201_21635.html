RespondentAnswer
\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1562784\n 1562784\n \n 1562784\n Minsky is an amazing lecturer who is really passionate about the mathematics that he teaches. Everything about the class was excellent and I always enjoyed coming to class even though it was difficult to understand some days. I do think that sometimes he rushes through some of the material and leaves some of the proofs we do in classes as exercises, but with the level of difficulty of the PSETs I think that it would be helpful if he gave those proofs so I could really have my foundation built up and I could focus on the proofs. On that note, some weeks some of the PSETs were grueling and I could not do some of the problems without the help of the ULA or Minsky in office hours. Not saying he should tone down the difficulty of the questions, but sometimes the techniques we need to use for the PSET problems are not talked about at all in class and basically come out of nowhere, so maybe for some of these questions in class Minsky could give a sort of introduction to the idea of the problem, or a similar problem, and start us off with the solution to help us out. Maybe just in general we could do more examples in class instead of just sort of building up the foundation in class and then having to apply it out of class. But again, the foundation was incredible and we could all eventually get the PSET questions done. This feedback just comes from my frustration spending so much time trying to figure out answers I never would have gotten without some help.
