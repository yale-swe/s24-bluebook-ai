\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1530380\n 1530380\n \n 1530380\n This course is the "leminar", meaning there is more interaction with the professor but no discussion section. I found the lack of built-in supports challenging at times, and relied heavily on supplemental textbook reading to cement my understanding of concepts covered in class. I think there was a peer tutor for this class, but they did not have office hours regularly. This class could be improved by having more opportunities for support built into the class structure (e.g., more office hours, regular availability of peer tutors). \r\n\r\nOtherwise, the class was structured in a clear and logical way and lectures were clear to follow. The information we learned was really interesting, and I especially appreciated how conceptual models were applied to real-life situations throughout the course.
\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1534372\n 1534372\n \n 1534372\n Wonderful engaging lecturer and material applicable to the real-world problems

Although the professor does not like everything in the book, I think it was a nicer text to have to accompany the lectures. My recommendation would just be to make it mandatory again.
