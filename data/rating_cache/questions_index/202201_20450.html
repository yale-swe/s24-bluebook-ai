There are three different places to access homework/classwork, and it is very hard to tell which assignment is posted where/due when/etc.\r\nThere is very little feedback. We had several grammar quizzes on subjects we had only been given a review sheet for on canvas. However, her grading is quite severe despite a lack of in class preparation.
