\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1615535\n 1615535\n \n 1615535\n Great class!
\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1615553\n 1615553\n \n 1615553\n The labs for this course complement the lecture course well, and the pre-lab lectures are helpful for understanding WHY we carry out the experiments we do. It would be helpful in future iterations of the course to update the lab manual and -- in the case of longer labs -- to shorten the number of tasks such that students can collect reliable data without rushing. It was also noted that students in the Tuesday section often did not get data as they were the first section of the week. By the time we got to the Wednesday section (my section), we had a better idea of how to move through the lab efficiently -- but at the cost of the Tuesday students. It would be helpful to go over lab protocols in more detail with the TFs beforehand and to confirm that the pacing is suitable for undergraduates. Additionally, it was quite strenuous to dissect the frog NMJ for four weeks in a row. I feel that three weeks of dissection is more than enough for us to learn the procedure and gather meaningful data. Lastly, while the three TFs in my section were outstanding, they were stretched too thin as nearly every lab bench needed assistance multiple times throughout the lab. In future years, recruitment of a fourth TF would be helpful.
