The material is also very complicated and may require several readings for someone to really understand. The course probably leaves you with more questions than answers which might be an issue if you go in expecting to figure out morality. The course is also driven a lot by the students in the course, so you may want to figure out who is in the class. Darwall wants to be as inclusive as possible but that seemed to result in some folks in our course who would have benefitted from a deeper philosophical background. That said, I enjoyed the course as a whole.
