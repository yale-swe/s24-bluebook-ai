\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1562654\n 1562654\n \n 1562654\n Strengths: \r\n- The professor is clearly very knowledgeable about the subject. I liked that we read directly from his book. \r\n- I really like the discussions at the end of every class, although the same students seemed to mainly participate every class. \r\n\r\nWeaknesses: \r\n- The grading seemed a bit arbitrary, especially since it's all done by the TA. Since we weren't given averages or letter grades, I had no way of interpreting my grades. More guidelines or distribution info would've been appreciated. \r\n- I think that pretty much no one did all the reading. Realistically with 3+ other classes, we probably can't read 40-60 pages in depth between each class, especially if we're discussing them in the next class. \r\n- I didn't love the format of the lecture itself. It just wasn't super engaging some of the time. I think it would be better if the discussions were a bit more interspersed and there was more of a norm of calling on students and asking questions throughout the lecture.
