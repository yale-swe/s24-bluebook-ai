\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1532321\n 1532321\n \n 1532321\n We learned about the dynamics of simple biological systems like ligand binding to receptor, gene expression under the control of positive or negative feedback (or feedforward), an introduction to stochasticity and noise in gene expression, and the dynamics of enzymatic activity and substrate cycles. The big themes were how to distill systems to simple mathematical models, how to analyze a system's steady-state behavior, and how decisions can be made at the cellular or molecular level.\r\n\r\nI gained a basic level of competence in MATLAB, but I'm not sure how applicable it would be to other areas. For the most part the coding was very simple (plotting based on equations we derived on paper, or having the program numerically integrate an ODE for us).

\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1543424\n 1543424\n \n 1543424\n I acquired a deep understanding of the biological mechanisms that are present in living organisms, as well as the clear methodology of reducing complicated circuits and systems into sets of equations that can easily be analyzed. I also learned how to code in Matlab as a means of analyzing these systems of equations and patterns in molecular biology.
