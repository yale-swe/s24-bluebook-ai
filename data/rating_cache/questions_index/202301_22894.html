This process removed a lot of the pressure I felt with writing, and it is very conducive to taking risks, which I believe is an invaluable aspect of improving your writing. I also thought the collection of pieces we read was a strength. They all felt very distinct and had different qualities to chew on. The class discussions about those pieces were also never dull. Finally, the actual class time was augmented with many writing exercises that were great for generating and playing with ideas, even if I never used them.\r\n\r\nNo weaknesses :)
