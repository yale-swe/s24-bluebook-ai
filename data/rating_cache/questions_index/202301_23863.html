\n \n Navigate to all answers for Respondent Id 1655798\n 1655798\n \n 1655798\n STRENGTHS: Professor Soles' Hamlet reading. The syllabus. WEAKNESSES: Professor Soles is a genius, but sometimes seminar felt more like lecture than seminar. Also feedback was incredibly extensive and more comprehensive than any other DS track, but it took many, many weeks to receive it.
