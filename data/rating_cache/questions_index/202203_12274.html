Foucault starts the course before it moves into tons of other great topics, like Black life and imagination, pandemic life, STS, trans life, debility and disability, life and work, and a lot more. Learned how to read better, think about the world a bit differently, and how to ask better questions. Learned a lot about different frameworks for problematizing the normative world, which is often daunting and difficult, but rewarding as well.

Even Haraway and cyborg theory was manageable. The style of doing one scholar for each class and following themes as we traveled through the theorists helped tie the class into one cohesive unit.
