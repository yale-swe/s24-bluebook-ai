Professor Valeggia is the best professor and made the space welcoming and inviting. She had us do a 5 min meditation at the start of each class to ground us and prepare us for the class ahead. The final project is really open and creative which allowed for students' to express individual and unique perspectives about what they learned in the course. Students led the discussion each week which provided a myriad of different perspectives as the starting point for each topic. Weaknesses: There was a good amount of reading that didn't end up getting discussed because there were so many interesting topics to talk about. This made doing some of the reading a chore because it wasn't discussed at all.
