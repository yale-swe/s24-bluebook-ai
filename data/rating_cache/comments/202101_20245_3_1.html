 Yes! Professor Thomasson ensures that this course is a joyful experience for all involved. She manages to present a huge amount of information about the Classical Era through lectures that include lots of interesting film clips. I really knew nothing about older films before taking this class, and I learned a ton! I wasn't sure if I would like black and white films, but I found them to be very engaging and watching them felt more like entertainment than work. I liked how flexible and unique the writing assignments were in this course. Instead of just having a bunch of traditional essays, we had two essays, a film diary, and a movie memory interview. This adds up to a lot of writing, though. The film diary, which you work on each week of the semester, is about 13 pages single-spaced. This course would be best for someone who likes movies and doesn't mind writing about them.
