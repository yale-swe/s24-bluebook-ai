 The greatest strength of the course is the discussion sections. Since the discussion sections are smaller (10-15 people), it allows for a lot of good substantive discussion about the material from our readings and lecture, giving us the opportunity to digest the material in our own terms. Discussion sections were really crucial in helping me understand some of the more difficult material in the class. The greatest weakness of the course are the readings. Some of the readings (especially some of the older ones) are a bit difficult to read and understand (because of the language and some missing context), and others are just very dry and/or technical, which makes getting through the readings and understanding them a little difficult at times. Some days also feature a lot more reading than others, which I feel like hinders my ability to understand all of the material from the texts that I've been made to read.
