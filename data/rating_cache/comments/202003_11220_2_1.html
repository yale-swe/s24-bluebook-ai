 Content is very interesting, but the timing for exams and quizzes didn't make sense. The quizzes were meant to be "check-ins" if you read the textbook chapter and watched lecture, but they pulled very small details and you had 12 minutes for 10 questions. They were open note but you ran out of time pretty much every single time because you were searching through your notes so much. The exams were also open note, but there were so many free response questions (with required sourcing) that you would barely finish. The grading is fair in the end, but the exams/quizzes create unnecessary stress.
