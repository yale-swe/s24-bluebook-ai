 I learned a lot about the topic at hand, and I also developed my skills of historical analysis specifically primary source analysis as well as critical reading of secondary sources.
 I learned about the construction and deconstruction of the slave regime from a political, economic and social standpoint. This class is a gateway into having a more keen understanding of the American context.

Skillwise, this course is a typical history lecture essays provide opportunities to practice directly analyzing primary sources, readings (secondary and primary) supplement lecture, and exams mostly test memorization and overall course themes.
