Â Strengths: Having two star professors teach such a course was a real privilege. I was hanging from every single thing that Professor Christakis was saying. He was by far the most engaging lecturer I have ever had at Yale. Professor Nordhaus was also really good but certainly did not have the same command of speech as Professor Christakis.
