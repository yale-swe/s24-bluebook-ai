2020-Summer PHYS S166 01 General Physics Laboratory

Q:

What are the strengths and weaknesses of this course and how could it be improved?

ID: SU103 CRN: 30271 Term: 202002

Back123Forward
 Strengths: 5 weeks, so it's short, you can get it done fast. spare yourself the trouble of prolonging it over the duration of a semester. Also I found that SPL can get really hot / has poor ventilation. Strength of this course: you can take physics lab in the comfort of your air-conditioned house. Another strength, I liked the PLA's, I liked that we could choose from a variety of different things to do. Weaknesses: it was online :( no way to actually do the experiments myself, I felt like i spent the entire course just writing, writing, writing nonstop. Improvements: send students lab kits! like little lenses / wires / mirrors or whatever. Snap circuits! Also, I wish the PLA's had some options for students who had equipment in their house. Like I have a voltmeter / multimeter and different kinds of wires in my basement, as well as prisms / lenses / mirrors / tuning forks (for resonance) lying around my house...
 I think that this course is very straightforward in regards to conveying the materials. One particular strength that I found in this course was that the professors really are passionate about teaching the material to students and super accommodating in regards to explaining confusing topics. I think that one area of improvement is the work expectations at some weeks, although I think that is largely due to this being an online summer course. Also, I found that some weeks the assignment were posted much too late to actually prepare for class and the lab quiz. Often the lab handouts were not released until like midnight or 12 hours before the class, and I didn't see them until I woke up in the morning.
 This course was good for an online lab class, which I understand is difficult. It is hard to watch the videos for lab while someone is speaking over Zoom because you cannot hear the videos if someone is talking. Perhaps we should watch the videos before class and come prepared with questions or find a way where we can mute zoom to watch videos.
 I think it was taught as well as it could possibly be taught given the circumstances. In the future would highly highly recommend taking it in person because we didn't get a chance to play around with a lot of cool physics toys.
 sometimes it was hard to visualize things through zoom and harder to explain and understand things
 More breakout rooms! I miss talking to people.
 Strengths: Not too hard Instructors were extremely available during class Weaknesses: Virtual Labs are hard to learn from Post-lab activities took way too long and did not teach me anything
 Fairly good course overall. Well adapted to online learning. Something the camera angle proved it was impossible to fully see what was going on, leading to questions we did not have all the information needed to solve.
 This course was great. Way more manageable than the regular semester because the course is much more inclusive and help is readily available.
 This semester felt a bit more repetitive in its experiments as compared to the previous semester of mechanics. This is because we were taking the same kind of data over and over again just on different systems or circuits. I understand that almost all of the previous labs were simply converted to an online format. If new labs could be thought of that are more conducive to the online format than the previous ones, that may be more helpful. I did appreciate the organization of the course. The videos are easy to follow and the lab manuals are easy to understand.
 Generally, I thought the course was well adapted to the online format. The labs covered a wide range of topics and were informative. However, the structure of lab sessions was inconsistent across sections. Some TAs walked students through each step of the lab, while others let students work independently. I think making the structure of the sessions more consistent would be helpful.
 Strength: Dedicated, hard-working faculty who are always available to help. Weakness: A bit last-minute on everything
 The strengths of this course was the hands on post lab activities. I enjoyed making my own working crystal radio set. Again I was very lucky to have my own materials, I think this course could be made more engaging for other students by providing them with materials to experiment Also sometimes the labs were posted very close to the actual lab time. Ideally it would have been nice to have more time to review the lab before it started.
