2018-Summer PSYC S110E 01 Introduction to Psychology

Q:

What are the strengths and weaknesses of this course and how could it be improved?

ID: SU103 CRN: 30188 Term: 201802

Back123Forward
 Having the class online was really interesting and very interactive. It was awesome to get to speak to professor Dunham personally and ask him direct questions. Way better than a big class lecture that can get banal at times
 The course was well organized and there were never issues with communication or assignment planning. The lectures we watched were engaging and the discussions went well.
 This course did a great job of clarifying the material we watched in lecture of the students. I have no complaints!
 Does a good job covering a significant breadth of information in a short time. Not much to be improved on, but was not a class that rocked my world.
 I wish there was more of an emphasis on social psychology. Besides that, the course was structured well relative to its length.
 I think there could be more of a group dynamic in the course. We once utilized breakout sessions on GoToMeeting to discuss things in smaller groups. I think this could be utilized more. I also, personally, did not learn that much from the Noba readings. They were full of too much information, and I think they can be omitted from the course entirely. Instead, I think more articles, both scholarly and from popular press, could be included.
 The strengths of this course is how it is structured, as well as an amazing professor to explain difficult topics very clearly. The only downside of this course is how much information is packed into the summer session, but it is still manageable.
 The major strength of this summer course was the small class size, which allowed for direct conversation with the professor, TF, and rest of the class. I also really appreciated the condensed nature of this course, since it was easier to remain engaged in the material and devote more energy into it.
 I loved the lectures and thought the assignments reinforced the material! The online discussions were a treasure. The only weakness was no captions on the videos :(
 The course was so well taught! Professor Dunham does an amazing job moving through so many topics in such a short period of time.
 This course put the amount of one semester studying into 5 weeks, which really makes it difficult for high students like me. The videos and homework are necessary for students to understand, but they may really not that interest students.
 Strengths: dedicated professor and TA, interesting content, intuitive platform Weaknesses: none
