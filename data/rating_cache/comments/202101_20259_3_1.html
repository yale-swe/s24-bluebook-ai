 Absolutely. If you are a premed student, do not become a doctor without taking this course, even if you are not interested in reproductive health. Even if you are not premed, this course is absolutely amazing and I guarantee that you will learn something new. The assignments were all very manageable and never seemed like busy work. I cannot recommend this course enough.
