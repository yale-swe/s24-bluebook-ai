2020-Summer GMAN S999E 01 German for Reading

Q:

What knowledge, skills, and insights did you develop by taking this course?

ID: SU100 CRN: 30168 Term: 202002

123Forward
 By reading the textbook and by interacting with the TAs I learned the basics of German.
 I learned about German grammar and developed a high-register, academic passive vocabulary.
 I learned about the grammatical structures of German, which helped me read German academic texts effectively.
 I developed a thorough, nuanced understanding of German grammar; at the end of the course, I am able to make my way through reasonably sophisticated German-language materials, with dictionary as needed. I was able to build on my previous knowledge of the language during this course: the vocabulary presented in the textbook, which was always glossed in the lectures, helped me to develop strategies to memorize, or at least digest, a great deal of content in a timely manner.
 German reading and translation. Also how to use dictionaries.
 German grammar (reading) - German grammar has very sophisticated verb conjugations which require lots of review and practice.
 reading knowledge of german
 I started with virtually zero knowledge of German and the course exceeded by far my expectations. The online course, highly structured yet somewhat flexible, allowed me to develop important reding skills and to translate, with the help of a dictionary, relatively difficult texts.
 I can recognize German words, detect the word order in a German sentence, and recognize the tense most of the time. For example, I was capable of understanding a couple of German book titles in a bibliography without using a dictionary last week.
 By taking this course, I ultimately accomplished what I set out do when I enrolled in the first place: I feel that my reading skills in German greatly improved during the brief period that the class was in session. I have a much more intuitive understanding of the way phrases in German are constructed, and am well on my way to developing an efficient approach to translation.
 I was able to read journal articles in my field with help from a dictionary by the end of the course
 Extensive knowledge of German grammar and syntax. My vocabulary has also grown significantly.
 Basic proficiency in German, and a sense of how the rhythm of the language works differently to English.
 l gained an improved understanding of German grammar, syntax and vocabulary and developed new skills for reading unseen German academic texts. The course provided me with a broad range of new tools to analyse and interpret complex German material without being fluent in German, which is immensely useful in my field. The course has also given me more confidence in approaching German texts.
