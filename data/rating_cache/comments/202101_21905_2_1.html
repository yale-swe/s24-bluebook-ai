We had to argue to get the professors to give us Sunday night as well, but then they only posted the exam around 9pm on Sunday. I was taking a physics quiz late at night every Sunday because these professors couldn't give us 10-15 minutes to complete the quiz in class. It was awful and I have no idea why these professors found it acceptable to foist this upon their students during a pandemic. I had no time to complete this quiz when you factor in how long we spent on problem sets each week and the time to understand the convoluted module notes.
