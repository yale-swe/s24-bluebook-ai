 Yes, I would absolutely recommend this course to another student. For MCDB majors or people interested in science, this course is a great core science class to take given its relatively okay workload.  Microbiology is a great course for anyone who is interested, and I would recommend it as someone who took it as a non-major. The professors make the material accessible to all and provide unique examples of interesting microbes that you wouldn't learn elsewhere. Understanding the sporulation of an anthrax relative, for example, was fascinating. In addition, I learned how to create causal studies-- a skill that I will carry forwards in the rest of my classes.
