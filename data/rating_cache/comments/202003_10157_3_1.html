 This class was the most forthcoming in its plan to increase accessibility for students in differing circumstances due to the pandemic. As with other classes, the midterm and final exams were allowed to be taken at any point throughout a 24 hour period. Additionally, our professor created an alternate exam format for students with additional needs registered with the office. Both exams were done in an open note, open internet format, with the only caveat being that no human aid was allowed. There were many avenues to submit assignments, and this was also done exceptionally well. I was able to transition to another submission method that suited my needs better with no issues. Our teaching fellow and TA's were always available, and in particular, our teaching fellow would lead two 'problem sesssions' where he would go over problems similar to the ones in that week's assignment (or exam). All in all, it was made very easy to have a successful semester in this class.
