 In depth ev
 We learned about different aspects of the 2008 Global Financial Crisis: its buildup and causes in the United States, the events of the crisis, how it spread to Europe, and different regulatory responses. We reviewed different types of sources about the crisis including empirical analyses, firsthand accounts of leaders during the crisis like Secretary Timothy Geithner, and books about the crisis. In learning about the crisis we learned about the financial system its relationship to the real economy as well as the current measures in place due to COVID-19 that may help prevent another financial crisis.
