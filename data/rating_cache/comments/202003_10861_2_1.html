When most people make an error, they accept it and correct for their mistakes; they announced this late enough in the term that they should've owned up to THEIR error and moved the due date to the 12th, instead of sending a snide email and giving us less time. 2.5) "Bad judgment" - to be frank, the prof used some outdated terms for several groups of minorities, that while they are not the "most" racist terms to be used, were done in poor taste. The readings were really interesting; that's about it.
