Q:

What are the strengths and weaknesses of this course and how could it be improved?

ID: SU103-SA CRN: 30253/30252 Term: 201902

Back123Forward
Â The main strength of the program is that it took advantage of the diversity that exists in Ecuador to facilitate real world learning. Through visiting many of the local communities, we were shown many different aspects of Andean culture and how they affect Ecuador today. The main weakness of the program was the lack of communication on many of our class trips. While I greatly enjoyed most of the trips, I often had little idea what we were driving to whenever we got onto the bus. This made it difficult to pack and difficult to plan the day. This could be improved by a daily announcement of some kind that informs the class what to expect for the day and what to bring. Additionally, we did not return to Quito until late Sunday night for two or three of our weekend trips. As I avoided bringing my laptop to the more remote regions of the country, it was extremely difficult to complete the work that was due for class on Monday.
Â I really enjoyed the trips and learning opportunities we had outside the classroom, but I found the coursework to be disorganized and not the most educational. Often times our assigned readings and in classwork seemed to be scattered in theme, lacking clear focus. A lot of the class centered on films, including interviews with prominent Ecuadorian filmmakers. While I really appreciated this opportunity to meet and talk with the artists, I think we lacked the knowledge about film theory and filmmaking to properly discuss these movies, especially in a Spanish. I would recommend more instruction about film theory, or better yet less of us interviewing the filmmakers and more of them presenting their work and explaining their artistic choices.

On some of the weekend trips, furthermore, we got back late Sunday night but still had class the next day, which seemed a little unfair considering the amount of homework that would accumulate over the weekend.
