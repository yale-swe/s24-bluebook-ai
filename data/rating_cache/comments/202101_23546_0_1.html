Prof. Snyder is a top class orator and explainer you will learn why self-determination is a double-edged sword, understand the rise of nationalist movements under parliamentarian governments, analyze authoritarianism from history and fiction, and gain a good grasp on the period from 1914-1995. Emphasis on the 1914-1956 period; after goulash communism, the wheels really fell off the wagon and Snyder began skipping through the 60s, 70s, 80s, and 90s because so much of the course was focused on the interwar period and WWII (which were interesting, but again poor course planning). You will also learn how to expertly skim 200+ pages of reading each week because the amount of reading assigned is unmanageable for a lecture course. Good luck with this one! Wouldn't recommend this unless Snyder realizes he should put his celebrity tour on pause and resume being a professor!
