2020-Summer ENGL S114 01 Writing Seminars I

Q:

Would you recommend this course to another student? Please explain.

ID: SU109 CRN: 30048 Term: 202002

Back123
 Yes!! This course has made me such a better writer and thinker. I'm going to use these skills for the rest of my college career, in any class where I have to write or discuss anything. It's so small that you really can know your instructor, and your TF. There is so much room to receive feedback and support. You also get to work with and meet other students on a personal level, which I really appreciated.
 Yes, absolutely! I think this course is excellent, especially for people who have not had a lot of writing experience in the past because it helps to polish even the basics (while also coaching students on more complex writing techniques and expectations). It is an excellent introduction to academic writing in college and sets a student up for more confident and compelling writing in future courses. The course is well structured to hone different writing skills, the regular feedback is very valuable, and the small class size ensures meaningful discussion. I finished this course genuinely feeling like I grew tremendously as a writer and enhanced my thinking ability.
 Yes would highly reccomend this course to others because of the strengths that I have previously mentioned.
 Yes, I would definitely recommend this course to another student! This course is very well structured in terms of teaching students how to persuasively and succinctly write each part of a college-level argumentative essay. The course topic "Life-Writing" is also intriguing and thought-provoking. By analyzing different types of life-writing texts (memoir, academic article, movie, and interview, etc), we understand the variety of materials that can constitute life-writing works and how specific choices of medium can help advance the audience's understanding of the authors' life stories. I find this deepened understanding of the various tracks of lives that others have gone through highly meaningful, as it allows me to rethink how I want to live and define my own life.
