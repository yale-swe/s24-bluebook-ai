Weaknesses: Middle half of course on petroleum/coal geology and the world's fossil fuel endowment felt slow and not as applicable to me. I wish we had really focused on environmental consequences/emissions and less on the origins of fossil fuels and geology. Lectures occasionally felt too broad with way too much granular detail. I sometimes had a hard time sensing what was most important and what was going to be tested on exams.
