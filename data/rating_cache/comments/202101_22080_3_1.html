 I would absolutely recommend this course to another student. South Africa is a complex yet beyond important country about which to learn. Moreover, Professor Magaziner's lectures effectively encapsulated a myriad of interpretations of the country's story. Never before have I encountered a history course that so fervently tried to analyze the past from every perspective while recognizing the limits of its own scope. Every class is, as Professor Magaziner stated on the first day of the course, "a" history, and his discussions of historiography in the context of South Africa's story were vital discussions for new and veteran history students alike. This class made me a better student and, I hope, a better critical participant of the world. In my opinion, it is a must-take here at Yale.
