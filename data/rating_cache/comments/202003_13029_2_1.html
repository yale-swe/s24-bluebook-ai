This led to a lot of stress and frustration with the course.

 Strengths: The topic of this class is interesting in and of itself. It assuaged some of the common fears people have with subliminal messages and unconscious behaviors (mostly towards the very end), and it guarantees that you will never be short of studies to cite concerning unconscious tendencies. Since there is no homework, I also appreciated having 3 tests, rather than 2. Weaknesses: This class was quite slow-going at times. I appreciate classes that recap previous lectures, so as not to lose students, and to ensure that every lecture is connected to a bigger theme. Sometimes, though, it felt as if every lecture was reiterating the same past few lectures. I'm not advocating for overloading students with extraneous information for midterms/finals, but by the end of the semester, I was left wondering how much more could have been covered if recaps didn't occur as often as they did.
